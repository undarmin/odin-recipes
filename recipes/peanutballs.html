Peanut Butter Balls

Description

Peanut butter balls are always a crowd-favorite with a hardened chocolate coating and a smooth, creamy, melt-in-your-mouth peanut butter filling. This recipe delivers an unexpected, but delightful crunch, thanks to the addition of crisp rice cereal. Decorate your peanut butter balls with sprinkles or other candy coatings just after dipping, or drizzle melted chocolate over top.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Rice Cereal
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Melt the peanut butter and butter in a saucepan. Mix the sugar and cereal together, then stir the cereal mixture into the peanut butter mixture.
  2. Form the mixture into balls, cover, and refrigerate until firm.
  3. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler. Coat the peanut butter balls in the melted chocolate. Refrigerate until the chocolate is firm.
